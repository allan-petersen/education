Method pattern 		
[Modifiers]	      Public fr example

[ClassType with package prefix]
MethodName (Arguments) [throws Exception Type]          *

ReturnType and MethodName with argumens are mandatory

*  matches one (return type, package, class methodname, argument)
.. matches zero or more(arguments or packages)
